NoesisFood

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how NoesisFood may process information to provide packaged food product analysis, including barcode or product lookup, nutrition insights, ingredient interpretation, allergen and dietary signals, safety and trust information, and corrections or feedback.

NoesisFood provides informational product analysis only. Results do not replace medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Please do not submit sensitive personal health information through the service.

1. Privacy Policy

NoesisFood is a packaged food product analysis service. This page describes, in general terms, how information may be processed where needed to provide, secure, maintain, review, and improve the service.

2. Information we process

NoesisFood may process information that you provide directly or that is generated through use of the service, including:

  • product identifiers such as barcodes or manual product references;
  • manual inputs, including nutrition values, product names, ingredient text, and related product information;
  • photos or scans used for product, ingredient, or nutrition analysis;
  • corrections, feedback, or review submissions;
  • technical logs, service usage data, and request metadata needed for operation, reliability, and security.

3. How we use information

We may use processed information where needed to:

  • deliver product analysis and related explanations;
  • support barcode, manual, and photo-based workflows;
  • provide allergen, dietary, safety, and usage-context information;
  • maintain service integrity, diagnose errors, and improve accuracy and performance;
  • review corrections and feedback that help improve the service over time.

4. Photos and scan data

If you use photo or scan features, NoesisFood may process uploaded image data and extracted text where needed to perform OCR, identify nutrition or ingredient information, or support analysis and troubleshooting. Please avoid uploading images that contain unnecessary personal information.

5. Product data and external sources

NoesisFood may use external product and safety data sources where needed to provide product lookup, ingredient or nutrition enrichment, and safety-related information. External sources may vary over time and may have their own data practices.

6. Feedback and corrections

If you submit corrections or feedback, we may process that information to review reported issues, improve analysis quality, and support product and service maintenance.

7. Technical logs and service usage data

Like most online services, NoesisFood may process technical logs and service usage data where needed for reliability, security, abuse prevention, debugging, monitoring, and operational analytics.

8. Data sharing and service providers

Information may be processed by hosting, infrastructure, or service providers acting on behalf of the service where needed to operate, secure, maintain, or improve NoesisFood. We may also query external product or safety information sources where needed to provide core functionality.

9. Data retention

Information may be retained for as long as reasonably needed for service operation, security, troubleshooting, quality improvement, compliance, and record-keeping. Retention may vary depending on the type of data, the operational purpose, and applicable requirements.

10. User rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights regarding access, correction, objection, restriction, or deletion. If you have a privacy-related request, please contact us using the email below.

For information about how to request deletion or correction of identifiable submissions, please see the Data Deletion page.

11. Children

NoesisFood is not intended for children to submit personal information independently. Users should not submit sensitive personal health information through the service.
